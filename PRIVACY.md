# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026
**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is handled in connection with services operated by Ian Forester ("we", "us", or "our"). This system operates as a personal AI assistant deployed for a single authorized user. It is not a public-facing product or service.

## What Data We Collect

The system may process the following categories of information:

- **Messages and queries** submitted by the authorized user through Telegram or the web interface
- **System event data** such as security alerts and automated notifications
- **Phone number** of the authorized user, used solely to deliver SMS notifications they have requested
- **Usage logs** for operational monitoring and debugging purposes

## How Data Is Used

Data is used solely to:

- Respond to user queries and requests
- Deliver SMS notifications and alerts to the registered user
- Monitor system health and security

## Data Sharing

**We do not share personal data with third parties. We do not use personal data for marketing purposes of any kind.**

All data remains within the user's own self-hosted infrastructure. No personal information is sold, rented, or disclosed to any external party.

Third-party services used in operation (Telegram, Anthropic, Twilio) receive only the minimum data necessary for their specific function and are governed by their own privacy policies.

## SMS Messaging

SMS messages are sent only to the single authorized user who configured the system and provided their own phone number. Messages are strictly limited to system notifications, alerts, and responses to user-initiated queries. No marketing messages are ever sent.

To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply **STOP** to any message.

## Data Retention

Message logs and operational data are retained only as long as necessary for system operation. No long-term data archiving is performed.
