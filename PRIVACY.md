# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026
**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is handled in connection with services operated by Ian Forester ("we", "us", or "our").

## Personal AI Assistant Service

This system operates as a personal AI assistant deployed for a single authorized user. It is not a public-facing product or service.

### Information Collected

The system may process the following categories of information:

- **Messages and queries** submitted by the authorized user through Telegram or the web interface
- **System event data** such as security alerts and automated notifications
- **Usage logs** for operational monitoring and debugging purposes

### How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to:

- Respond to user queries and requests
- Generate system alerts and notifications
- Monitor system health and security

### Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties. All data remains within the user's own infrastructure (self-hosted on their personal cloud instance).

Third-party services used in operation (Telegram, Anthropic, Twilio) are governed by their own privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is transmitted to these services beyond what is inherent to their normal operation.

### Data Retention

Message logs and operational data are retained only as long as necessary for system operation. No long-term data archiving is performed.
